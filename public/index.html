Non-Custodial

Your private keys never leave your device. Transactions are signed locally by your agent and submitted directly to Solana.

Jupiter Powered

Best-in-class swap routing through Jupiter aggregator. Optimal prices across all Solana DEXs with configurable slippage.

Privacy Native

No wallet tracking, no analytics on your trades. We return the transaction, you sign and send. Zero data retention on swap details.

Quick Start

curl -X POST https://agents.veertx.com/v1/swap \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer vrtx_live_..." \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "inputToken": "SOL",
    "outputToken": "USDC",
    "amount": "100000000",
    "agentPublicKey": "YourSolanaPublicKey...",
    "slippageBps": 50
  }'
